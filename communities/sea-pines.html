Sea Pines Resort

Sea Pines Resort represents the gold standard of Hilton Head Island living. Spanning 5,200 pristine acres of oceanfront property, this legendary destination blends resort-style amenities with genuine community living. Home to the iconic Harbour Town Lighthouse and three championship golf courses—including Harbour Town Golf Links, which hosts the prestigious RBC Heritage PGA Tour event—Sea Pines offers an unparalleled lifestyle.

Residents enjoy direct access to Calibogue Sound and pristine beaches, world-class dining and shopping at Harbour Town, a full-service marina, and championship-caliber tennis facilities. The community's private gates and security ensure exclusivity while maintaining the vibrant energy of a premier resort destination. Sea Pines is where oceanfront elegance meets timeless charm.

Properties in Sea Pines command premium valuations, ranging from $500K for entry-level residences to $10M+ for waterfront estates and oceanfront villas. The community attracts global luxury buyers seeking the ultimate Lowcountry experience.
