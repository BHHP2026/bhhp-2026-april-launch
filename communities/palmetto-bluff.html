Palmetto Bluff

Palmetto Bluff is a 20,000-acre masterpiece sprawling along the pristine May River in Bluffton. This exclusive community represents the pinnacle of Lowcountry luxury, combining world-class resort amenities, championship golf, and timeless Southern elegance. The centerpiece is the five-star Montage Palmetto Bluff resort, offering residents and guests unparalleled hospitality and lifestyle services.

The community features a Jack Nicklaus Signature golf course consistently ranked among the best in the Carolinas, deep-water marina access for yacht owners, an extensive equestrian center with pristine trails, and carefully preserved nature preserves that protect the region's ecological integrity. Palmetto Bluff's village center offers curated dining, retail, and cultural experiences in an Old World, European-inspired setting.

Properties range from $700K to $8M+, encompassing riverfront estates, golf-course residences, and historic plantation-style homes. Palmetto Bluff attracts discerning buyers seeking the confluence of natural beauty, sophisticated amenities, and an intimate yet dynamic community lifestyle.
